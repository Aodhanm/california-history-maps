Native Peoples Index

Native nations and named individuals as they appear in the sources plotted on these maps, with links to every feature that names them. The identifications follow the sources and the project's register; where a source names only "gentiles" of a place, no nation is attributed. This index grows as the maps do. The Native California map shows the approximate homelands of these and the other nations of Alta California.
